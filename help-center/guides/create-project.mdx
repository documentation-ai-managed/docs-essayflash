## Overview

Start organizing your writing quickly in EssayFlash.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Dashboard">
    Navigate to `https://app.essayflash.com/dashboard`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Select the "New Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter project details">
    Fill in your project title and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start writing">
    Click "Create" to begin drafting your essay.
  </Step>
</Steps>
