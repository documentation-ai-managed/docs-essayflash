---
title: Cannot Generate Essay
description: Solve issues when essay generation fails.
---

## Symptoms

- "Generation failed" message on EssayFlash
- Essay does not appear after submitting prompt

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure your device is online.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Reload EssayFlash and try the prompt again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-login to EssayFlash">
    Log out, then log back in to refresh your session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">If the problem persists, contact support@essayflash.com</Callout>
