## Overview

Creating an account lets you start using EssayFlash features.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://app.essayflash.com/register`
2. Enter your email and choose a password
3. Click "Sign Up"
4. Check your inbox and verify your email

Once verified, log in and complete your profile.

Need help? Email support@essayflash.com
</Callout>
