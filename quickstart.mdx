## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Google account for single sign-on

</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">

EssayFlash works best at resolutions `>1024x768` pixels. Disable ad blockers if you encounter loading issues.

</Callout>

## Sign Up and Log In

Create your account using one of these methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">

Follow these steps to sign up with email:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [https://app.essayflash.com](https://app.essayflash.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and agree to terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">

Use Google for faster signup:

1. Go to [https://app.essayflash.com](https://app.essayflash.com).
2. Click **Continue with Google**.
3. Select your Google account and authorize EssayFlash.
4. You're automatically logged in.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, add a description, and select visibility (`Public` or `Private`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Choose a slug (e.g., `my-project-docs`) for the URL: `https://docs.essayflash.com/my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click **Create Space**. You'll land in the editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Organize Initial Documents

Populate your space quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document** and name it `Introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Content">
    Use the editor to add frontmatter and content:

````mdx
---
title: Introduction
description: Welcome to My Project
---
## Overview

Start writing here.
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize">
    Create folders: Drag docs into **New Folder** like `guides/` or `api/`.
    Use the sidebar to nest pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** to make changes live. Preview first.
  </Step>
</Steps>
